Jiansen Song

Institute of Software Chinese Academy of Sciences

Beijing, China

My name is Jiansen Song (宋建森 in Chinese). I am currently a fifth-year Ph.D. student of Technology Center of Software Engineering (TCSE) at Institute of Software, Chinese Academy of Sciences (ISCAS). I am fortunate to be advised by Prof. Wensheng Dou, Prof. Jun Wei and Prof. Wei Wang.

I am interested in distributed databases. More recently, I focus on testing the reliability of relational databases via fuzzing.
